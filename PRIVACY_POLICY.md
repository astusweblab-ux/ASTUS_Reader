# Privacy Policy

ASTUS Reader is an Android reading and text-to-speech application developed by ASTUS LAB WEB STUDIO.

## Data Collection

ASTUS Reader does not collect, sell, or share personal data with external servers.

## Local Data

The app stores the following data locally on the user's device:

- imported books and extracted text;
- book covers extracted from imported files;
- bookmarks;
- reading progress;
- text-to-speech progress;
- reader and playback settings.

This data is used only to provide the app's reading and playback features.

## Files

ASTUS Reader imports books only after the user selects files through Android's Storage Access Framework. The app does not scan the device storage automatically.

## Text To Speech

Text-to-speech playback is handled by the TTS engine installed on the user's device, such as Google TTS, Samsung TTS, or RHVoice. The privacy behavior of the selected TTS engine is controlled by that engine's provider.

## Notifications

The app may show playback notifications while text-to-speech is active. Notification permission is used only for playback controls.

## Backups

Depending on Android system settings, local app data may be included in Android device backup or transfer features.
